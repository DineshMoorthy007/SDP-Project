

|**Aspect**|**Agile Methodologies**|**Waterfall Methodology**|
|-|-|-|
|Development Style|Iterative and Incremental|Linear and sequential|
|Requirement Changes|Easily accommodate changes|Changes are diff. once a phase is completed|
|Customer Involvement|Continuous Involvement|Limited involvement|
|Delivery|Frequent delivery of working software|delivery only at the end (\*drawback)|
|Testing|Continuous testing|Testing only after implementation phase|
|Documentation|Lightweight  Documentation|Heavy and detailed|
|Flexibility|Highly Flexible|Rigid and Inflexible|
|Risk Handling|Lower risk due to early feedback|Higher Risk|
|Team Structure|goes functional ,self organized teams|Clear defined|
|Suitable Projects|Large complex and evolving projects|small project with stable requirements|
